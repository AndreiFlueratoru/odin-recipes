Cuba Libre recipe

Description

The Cuba Libre cocktail is one of the most popular cocktails around today. The Cuba Libre recipe calls for rum, Coca-Cola, and lime juice. It is most commonly served in a highball glass with ice, and it has a sweet, citrusy and woody taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces rum.
  • 1 lime wedge.
  • Coca-Cola, to top.
  • Garnish: lime wheel.

Steps

  1. Fill a highball glass with lots of ice.
  2. Squeeze and drop 2 lime wedges into the glass, coating the ice well with the juices
